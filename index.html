How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same un-British item touch, it is annihilated! Keep destroying the un-British things until we are left with the One True Farage tile.

Un-British things:

Europe
UKIP believes that Britain is still a global superpower that can do better on its own. 8 out of 10 business owners disagree.
Immigrants
Coming over here, stealing our jobs and our benefits! Except the evidence says immigration is good for the economy. And let's not forget that free migration is also good for the 1.8 million Britons living elsewhere in the EU.
Same-sex couples
UKIP is very unclear with its position on same-sex marriage, having previously opposed it. A ban on same-sex marriages would enforce an "us and them" mentality by forcing same-sex couples to have a different legal status. And we needn't mention the "gay floods" incident.
Women
UKIP continues to iterate that women of child-bearing age are "worth less" to employers. A UKIP government would no doubt allow employers to favour male staff purely for being male.
The environment
UKIP is skeptical of climate change, despite overwhelming evidence, and would drop all renewable energy programmes to mine fossil fuels in the UK.
Equality
UKIP opposes political correctness, which is another way of saying they oppose equality measures. A UKIP government would have no problems abolishing disability, gender, sexual orientation and other equality protections for commercial businesses and employers.
The world
UKIP genuinely believes that British people are more worthy of aid than other human beings elsewhere, despite Britain having more wealth per person than 87% of countries in the world.
Peace
UKIP wants to dramatically increase our military budget, including purchasing three new aircraft carriers.
Human rights
A UKIP government would remove us from the European Convention on Human Rights. After that point the government will be answerable to no one and can introduce any laws they like, from reinstating the death penalty to criminalizing opposition to UKIP policy.
UKIP politicians
Any UKIP politician who says anything controversial is quickly evicted from the party. One wonders how many people will be left to run the country if they win.
